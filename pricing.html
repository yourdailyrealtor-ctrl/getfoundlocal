How the Pricing Works

Every website has two costs. First, a one-time build fee — this covers design, development, and getting your site live. Second, a monthly management plan — this covers your domain registration, hosting, and ongoing support. Both are required. The monthly plan is what keeps your site on the internet. Without it, your site will eventually go offline.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Do I own the website?+

Yes — fully. The domain is registered in your name and all files are yours to keep. The monthly plan covers hosting and maintenance, not ownership of the site itself.

Do I pay before seeing the site?+

No. We build your site first and send you a preview link. You do not pay anything until you have seen it and approved it. If you are not happy, you owe nothing.

What happens if I cancel the monthly plan?+

You can cancel anytime with no contracts or fees. Your domain will eventually expire and the site will go offline, but we will send you all your files first so you always have them.

How long does it take to go live?+

Usually 3–4 days from approval to a live site at your own domain. Most agencies take 4–6 weeks. We move fast because the process is dialed in.

Can I provide my own photos?+

Absolutely. Just send them over and we will use them. We can also pull photos directly from your Google Maps listing if that is easier.
